AFR Rich List 2019: Anthony Pratt is Australia's wealthiest person


Paper and packaging tycoon Anthony Pratt has been named Australia's wealthiest person for the third-straight year.

The Visy Industries boss tops the 2019 Australian Financial Review Rich List with a fortune of $15.57 billion, up from $12.9 billion last year.
